Help Center

Find answers to common questions

How do I start a new assessment?

Go to New Assessment from the sidebar and click on Start New Assessment.

How can I request more assessments?

Open the Request Assessment page and submit the request form with your reason.

Where can I see my past performance?

You can check scores and history in the Past Performance section from the sidebar.

My assessment is not starting. What should I do?

Please refresh the page or log out and log in again. If the issue persists, contact support.

How do I update my profile?

Go to Settings from the sidebar and update your personal information.
